Tuna Onigiri from Spirited Away

Simple, healthy little that is sure to lighten your day!

Sourced from: https://pen-online.com/food/recipe-for-tuna-onigiri-from-spirited-away/

Ingredients

150-200g jasmine rice
4 tbsp white vinegar
Salt
Sugar
1 tin regular tuna
500g mayonnaise
4 sheets nori seaweed


Directions

1. Wash the rice throughly in a pan. Drain and rinse until the water is clear. Tip: the volume of water needs to be half a finger greater than that of the rice. Cook the rice.



2. Once the rice is cooked, pour in the white vinegar and mix with a little salt and sugar.



3. Break up the tuna in a bowl and mix well with some mayonnaise, to taste.



4. Dampen your hands and create 4 'pancakes' using the rice. Place a flat layer of tuna on each one, then another flat layer of rice on top. Make them into balls, then shape the corners to form triangles.



5. Moisten the seaweed sheets, then place them under the onigiri.
